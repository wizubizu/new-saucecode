Welcome to Saucecode Hackathon

Saucecode is a project aimed at discovering and celebrating the finest tech talents in Africa starting with Nigeria. it was conceived to challenge the talents in our ecosystem, encourage programming best practise, promote collaboration, make new role models

Get Started     

The Competition

Teams consisting of a maximum of four people and a minimum of 2 will compete to be the best top-notch technical team in Nigeria. On signifying their interest, Participants would form teams to solve one from many of the listed problems and build their prototype in 4 weeks. Please check timeline for more information.

The top 10 teams with the highest votes make it to the next round where judges will select the five(5) teams to advance to the finals. These five(5) teams will then compete gainst each other by pitching to the judges at the finals.
